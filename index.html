Who are Vox Pockets?

Vox Pockets are a 6 piece covers band based primarily around Derbyshire within the Midlands of the UK. Playing hits from across the decades which are guaranteed to fill the dancefloor!

Comprised of highly skilled, experienced musicans (who happen to be good friends), Vox Pockets focus on providing a "feel good" vibe and keeping the energy up to get the good times flowing

For a truly unique, exciting and fun filled night, book Vox Pockets for your venue or event today!

What do Vox Pockets play?

Vox Pockets play a wide mix of songs to get your feet moving. With modern and classic hits that are a breath of fresh air from the same songs you may have heard time and time again!

Ranging from older classics like The Monkees and Cliff Richard up to some 70s and 80s hits by artists such as ABBA, Queen and Aha. With these firm classics on-board, Vox Pockets still don't shy away from more modern hits, songs by Walk The Moon, Coldplay and Harry Styles are all on the menu too!
